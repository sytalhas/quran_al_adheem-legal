# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 14, 2026
**Last updated:** April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Qordova Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the **Quran Al-Adheem** mobile application (the "App"). Qordova Inc. is a company incorporated in Manitoba, Canada.

## 1. Summary

**The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information about you.** There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no telemetry. All of your reading progress, bookmarks, learning data, and preferences are stored only on your device.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, and we do not have access to, any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information
- Your location
- Your device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP address (except as incidentally handled by third-party content providers — see Section 4)
- Usage statistics, analytics events, or behavioral data
- Crash reports or diagnostic logs
- Contacts, photos, microphone audio, or any other content on your device

The App requests no login and creates no account.

## 3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored **only** on your device, under the App's private storage area, and is never sent to us or to any third party:

- Your reading and listening progress in the Qur'an
- Bookmarks, notes, and reading preferences
- Spaced-repetition learning state (for the Al-Kalimāt vocabulary feature)
- App settings (reciter selection, font size, translation choice, etc.)
- Cached Qur'an recitation audio that you have played or downloaded

This information is automatically removed when you uninstall the App. We have no ability to recover it on your behalf.

## 4. Third-Party Content Providers

To function, the App connects to the following third-party servers to download publicly available Qur'anic content. These connections are made directly from your device to the third party; we never see or intermediate the traffic.

- **download.quranicaudio.com** — Qur'anic recitation audio files. Operated by Quranic Audio.
- **quran.com** — Qur'anic font assets used for rendering Arabic script. Operated by Quran.com / Tarteel AI.

When your device contacts these services, those services will necessarily see your device's IP address and the specific file being requested, as is the case with any internet request. We do not control these third parties and we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies if you have concerns. We do not send them any personal information about you; we only issue standard HTTPS file requests.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is a Qur'anic reference and learning tool suitable for users of all ages, including children. Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe that, despite the above, we have somehow received information from a child, please contact us at the address in Section 9 and we will investigate.

## 6. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information, because we do not collect any information to begin with.

## 7. Security

Because no personal information leaves your device, there is nothing for us to secure on our end. Data stored locally by the App is protected by the sandboxing and encryption features of your device's operating system (iOS or Android).

## 8. Your Rights

If you are located in a jurisdiction with data protection laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA, Canada), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), or similar, those laws grant you rights of access, correction, deletion, and objection regarding your personal data. Because we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing for us to retrieve, correct, or delete on your behalf. You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document and post the new version at the same URL. Material changes will be announced in the App's release notes. Your continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
