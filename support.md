# Support

Thank you for using **Quran Al-Adheem** by Qordova Inc.

## Contact

If you have a question, a bug report, a feature request, or general feedback, please email us:

**[info@mawadda.app](mailto:info@mawadda.app)**

We read every message and usually reply within 2 business days.

## Reporting a bug

To help us fix issues quickly, please include:

1. What you were doing when the problem happened
2. What you expected to happen
3. What actually happened
4. Your device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro, Pixel 8)
5. Your OS version (e.g. iOS 18.2, Android 14)
6. The app version (shown in the app's Settings screen)
7. A screenshot if possible
